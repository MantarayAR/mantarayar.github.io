What is Paugme?

Paugme is an interactive virtual pal that helps teach empathy to children with mid to high level functioning autism. It is directed towards elementary and middle school age students.

Teaching Emotions
and Actions

Paugme focuses on helping those with autism identify emotional expressions; but, beyond that, we try to help reinforce appropriate responses to emotions.
