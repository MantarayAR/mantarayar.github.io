What is Mantaray AR: Unlimited?

Teachers and parents can create their own Augmented Reality experience with little to no programming knowledge. It's as simple as creating a slide show presentation!

Teach with AR

Our Unlimited product allows you create unique Augmented Reality experiences, set up sessions for students to join, and track student progress.

Learn with AR

Is your teacher using Mantaray AR: Unlimited? Download the mobile app for students to join Augmented Reality teaching sessions! Your teacher will provide you with a Session Code to join your teacher's AR Experience.
