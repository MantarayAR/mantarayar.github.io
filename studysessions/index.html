Use Augmented Reality in the Classroom

Mantaray AR is a start-up that aims to create educational applications using Augmented Reality.

We are currently working on two distinct product lines: Study Sessions and Mantaray AR Unlimited. The Study Sessions are individual applications that will be downloadable from the App Store and the Google Play Store; Mantaray AR Unlimited will be an online application that allows teachers and others who are interested to create their own Augmented Reality experiences!

Get Students Engaged

Mantaray AR focuses on making sure that we teach as well as engage students in the material they are learning.

Augmented Reality helps us achieve this goal since it changes students from passive listeners to being actively engaged in the educational process.
